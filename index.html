Quadcopter Drone with LIDAR-based Spontaneous Geo-Spatial Mapping

This project involves designing and deploying an advanced quadcopter drone, controlled by joystick and hand gestures. Key features include vibration reduction for improved stability, a custom flight controller, and LIDAR integration for enhanced mapping. The drone will operate indoors without relying on GPS

First-Person View Radio-Controlled Car with Autonomous Driving

The project aims to design and build a self-driving first-person view (FPV) RC car that navigates autonomously while providing real-time video feedback to the operator. This platform integrates computer vision, machine learning, and robotics to explore autonomous vehicle concepts, offering an educational tool for experimentation in AI and robotics.
